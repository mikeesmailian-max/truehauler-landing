How It Works

  1. Post your load – You assign the carrier as usual in your TMS.
  2. Driver gets secure SMS link – TrueHauler automatically sends a browser-based verification link (no app install required).
  3. Live photo + GPS capture at shipper – Driver takes live selfie and truck photos with geofenced GPS validation at the pickup location.
  4. Broker approves identity – You review the photos and GPS in real-time. Approve or reject before releasing the pickup number.
  5. Pickup number released – Once verified, TrueHauler securely shares the pickup number with the confirmed driver.
  6. Optional: Real-time tracking + POD capture – Track the shipment and capture proof of delivery photos automatically.

Result: Block fake carriers, load phishing, and double brokering before pickup – without slowing down your operation.
